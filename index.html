How I got here

I started writing Skript to fix small annoyances on servers I played on — a vanish command that leaked join messages, a punishment flow that took too many steps. Two years later that habit turned into a full staff suite running GUIs, risk scoring, and live event systems.

Along the way I picked up Java and Kotlin to go deeper than Skript's ceiling, and rounded that out with general full-stack development. I like tools that disappear into the background and just work — that's the standard I hold my own code to.
